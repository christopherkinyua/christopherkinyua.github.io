The TA

This semester in our Integrated Learning Stream(ILS) we were tasked with building an audio device. Our group came up with an audio temperature sensor which we called the TA.

This project has gone through a lot of changes. At first it was intended to check if you happened to leave your refrigerator door open and alarm you if you did so your food does not go bad. We then changed it to our current idea which monitors the temperature of a plant and also the moisture levels of the soil. This product can be targeted for a lot of audiences such as green house owners who want to monitor the temperature of their plants and your everyday household if they want to monitor the temperature of their houses of their home plants.

Our project has gone through multiple changes. It was initially supposed to be a sign language to speech convertor. However, we changed it to a morse code to speech convertor then a remote forest fire alarm and finally to what we have. This was due to our poorly constructed user stories and we also the feasiblilty of the products were not satisfactory to us.

Teamwork

Through this project I have been able to develop various teamwork skills improving my work ethic. I have become more flexible as we are working with people form various time zones and we need to accommodate for them. I have also been able to take my own initiative so we can get done with some parts of the project without needing to be told by someone.

We encountered a number of challenges as we developed our product. It was difficult to build the product remotely as one person would have the parts making it difficult for the rest of us to debug any problem that would have occured for our other member who doesnt have the products. We solved this through daily-meetings and also due to change of the deliverables we focused more on the software aspect of the product and ran simulations to see if it would theoretically work.

All this was reminiscent to some of the projects that I had done before hand and despite the previous experience, I found it a lot harder to carry on the values that I had gained. Some of the challenges I faced was lack of information surrounding the project. I had to do lots of research in order to gain some understanding and move forward with its development. Due to Covid and a lot of unavoidable circumstances, we were not able to physically idealize this project as we only managed to come up with schematics. I am excited so actually build this project so I can satisfy my desire and curiousity of whether our project works using the schematics we have come up with.
