ENEL 300 Course

Design Process

During this class we learned about software development and project management. We incorporated the Agile process of program management which I have found to be very useful. It allows a lot of room for change and modification compared to more rigid and robust methods like the waterfall method which is hard to incorporate new ideas once the design process has already started. We are also able to preform multiple revisions to make the project better.

We used the SCRUM framework where we split the project into various sections with certain deliverables called sprints. This format gave us a lot of flexibility when it came to working through our project. Due to the current way things are due to COVID, this really helped us as we came across some unavoidable obstacles which we were able to get around due to its flexibility.

One of the many things that I took away from all of this was communication. It was very important to communicate amongst ourselves so we could achieve the set deadlines. We hosted daily meetings where we would discuss what we have done, what obstacle we have reached and how to move forward.

Entrepreneurship

This course has given me an opportunity to listen to a lot of creative minds of the students in the electrical engineering faculty. I have heard lots of crazy ideas and some ideas that tackle crucial matters affecting various parts of the society. This has helped improve my view on things as a future entrepreneur.

Throughout this course, we have been drilled into us skills to make us entrepreneurs. Some of the exercises we constantly carried out are:

  • Making User Stories
  • Making pitches
  • Making a business plan

By creating user stories, I am able to view our product from the users perspective and see if it is a viable product and how it plays a part in their day to day lives. With a user story, it helps guide the produc development and have a target audience for it.

The pitches have helped build confidence and brush up our presentation skills. It has helped me improve my delivery of ideas that I have and to push forward our product to our users and sponsors.
