Reflection

Looking back on this schedule that I set up for myself, I made major changes during the semester. I removed a lot of time waster such as gaming and put that time into more productive activities like studying and into my hobby, photography, which was a great way to get my mind off studies and take a break. I managed to keep up with my religious activites and always made time to attend mass. As the esmester went by my sleep time went down so as to cater for the increased work load. To reduce my time in the kitchen I started meal prepping so I had more time to study which then put me ahead of my classes and helped me catch up.
